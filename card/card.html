The bears

Bears eat mostly meat and fish, but some bears also eat plants and insects.

  • Scientific Name: Ursidae
  • Average Length: 120 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Northern Hemisphere

he Bear is a well-known animal. It is a big animal with either lighter or darker brown fur. Tail is short and not well seen among its fur. The young have a white collar around their neck, sometimes the adults also still have it. Bears walk like humans do on their soles. It has dull teeth as all these who eat both plants and animals. The Brown Bear is a common animal in Estonia and it has become more numerous in the current century. There are approximately 500 of them here.
